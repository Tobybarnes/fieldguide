# 50 Potential Projects for Field Guide

**Organized by Whole Earth Catalog principles:** Access to tools, self-reliance, learning, appropriate technology, community, craft.

---

## Personal Knowledge & Research (10 projects)

### 1. Reading Logger
Track books you're reading with notes, quotes, and connections between ideas. Export to your blog or share with friends. Not Goodreads (which is for displaying). For actual learning.

### 2. Research Notebook
Multi-agent system for deep research. Spawn agents to investigate different aspects of a topic. Synthesize findings. Export formatted reports. For writers, students, curious people.

### 3. Zettelkasten Builder
Personal knowledge graph. Connect atomic notes. Surface connections you didn't notice. Export subgraphs as articles. Based on Niklas Luhmann's method.

### 4. Source Tracker
Track where you learned things. "Where did I read about that Buddhist concept?" Links back to books, articles, conversations. Memory aid for learning.

### 5. Learning Path Generator
Pick a topic. Agent researches learning resources. Builds sequenced curriculum. Tracks your progress. Adapts based on what you've learned.

### 6. Quote Collector
Save quotes with context. Tag by theme. Surface relevant quotes when writing. Export collections. For writers and thinkers.

### 7. Concept Map Generator
Take your notes on a topic. Agent builds visual concept map showing relationships. Export as diagram. For understanding complex systems.

### 8. Bibliography Manager
Track sources for writing projects. Auto-format citations. Check what you haven't read yet. Export bibliographies. For researchers.

### 9. Study Group Coordinator
For book clubs or learning groups. Track readings, schedule discussions, collect member notes. Synthesize group insights. Not Slack (too noisy). For focused learning.

### 10. Field Notes Digitizer
Photograph handwritten notes. OCR to text. Organize by date and topic. Search across years of notebooks. For people who think on paper.

## Writing & Creation (8 projects)

### 11. Essay Incubator
Collect fragments and ideas. Agent suggests connections. Build outlines from fragments. Export drafts. For writers who think by writing.

### 12. Newsletter Composer
Pull from your link catcher, reading notes, and journal. Agent drafts newsletter synthesizing your recent thinking. You edit and publish. For people with something to say.

### 13. Blog Post Generator from Conversations
Record conversations (with permission). Transcribe. Agent extracts key ideas. Draft blog post. You edit. For people who think out loud.

### 14. Writing Feedback System
Submit draft. Agents read from different perspectives (clarity, argument, voice). Synthesize feedback. You decide what to revise. For improving your writing.

### 15. Story Idea Generator
Collect observations, overheard dialogue, interesting situations. Agent suggests story premises combining elements. For fiction writers.

### 16. Poetry Form Checker
Write poems. Check form constraints (haiku syllables, sonnet rhyme scheme). Suggest revisions. For poets learning traditional forms.

### 17. Editing Checklist Generator
Agent analyzes your writing patterns. Builds custom editing checklist based on your common issues. Updates as you improve. For self-editors.

### 18. Publication Tracker
Track submissions to journals/publications. Note responses. Agent suggests where to submit based on your work and their acceptance patterns. For writers seeking publication.

## Teaching & Sharing (6 projects)

### 19. Course Outline Builder
Rough idea for a course. Agent researches topic, suggests structure, identifies key concepts, proposes exercises. You refine. For people with knowledge to share.

### 20. Tutorial Generator
Build something. Document as you go. Agent generates tutorial with screenshots, code snippets, explanations. For sharers of knowledge.

### 21. Workshop Planner
Topic and timeframe. Agent suggests activities, timing, materials needed. You adapt to your style. For facilitators.

### 22. Learning Assessment Creator
Define what students should learn. Agent generates questions testing understanding (not memorization). For teachers.

### 23. Explanation Simplifier
Write expert-level explanation. Agent suggests simpler version for beginners. You refine. For people translating complexity.

### 24. Example Generator
Teaching a concept. Agent generates relevant examples from different domains. You pick the best. For teachers and writers.

### 25. FAQ Builder
Topic you teach often. Agent generates common questions based on your content. You write answers. Auto-updates as questions come in. For experts.

### 26. Student Progress Tracker
Small-scale teaching. Track what each student has learned, where they're stuck. Agent suggests personalized next steps. For tutors and mentors.

## Community & Connection (6 projects)

### 27. Gift Idea Collector
Note things people mention wanting/needing throughout the year. Agent surfaces relevant items near birthdays. For thoughtful gift-giving.

### 28. Conversation Starter Generator
Track topics friends care about. Agent suggests conversation starters based on their interests and recent events. For meaningful connection.

### 29. Gratitude Tracker
Daily practice noting what you're grateful for. Weekly synthesis showing patterns. Share with accountability partner. For mindfulness practice.

### 30. Skill Share Coordinator
Group of friends. Track who knows what, who wants to learn what. Agent suggests teaching/learning matches. Schedule skill shares. For informal education.

### 31. Local Event Tracker
Track local events, shows, talks, workshops. Agent suggests based on your interests. Share with friends. For engaging with your place.

### 32. Correspondence Logger
Track when you last wrote to people. Agent suggests who to reach out to. Draft letter starters. For maintaining long-distance friendships.

### 33. Book/Tool Lending Library
Track what you've lent to whom. Remind when overdue. Suggest lending based on friends' interests. For generous sharing.

### 34. Recipe Sharing Network
Small group recipe exchange. Track what you've tried from others. Rate and note modifications. Agent suggests recipes based on ingredients. For cooking communities.

## Health & Physical Practice (7 projects)

### 35. Symptom Journal
Track health symptoms, energy, mood. Identify patterns over time. Export for doctor visits. For understanding your body.

### 36. Mobility Practice Tracker
Track stretching/mobility work. Video form checks. Progress photos. Agent suggests variations when you plateau. For movement practice.

### 37. Sleep Pattern Analyzer
Log sleep quality, wake time, energy next day. Identify patterns (coffee after 2pm, screen time before bed). For better rest.

### 38. Meditation Timer + Logger
Flexible meditation timer. Log sessions with notes. Track consistency without gamification. For meditation practice.

### 39. Nature Walk Logger
GPS track walks. Photo observations. Note bird sightings, plant IDs, seasonal changes. Agent suggests nearby trails. For naturalists.

### 40. Garden Planner
Track what you planted, when, where. Weather data. Harvest notes. Agent suggests next season's planting. For gardeners.

### 41. Sobriety Support Tool
Track sober days. Note triggers and coping strategies. Agent surfaces your own wisdom when struggling. Private, no social features. For recovery.

## Financial & Resource Management (5 projects)

### 42. Expense Categorizer
Photo receipts. OCR and categorize. Monthly reports. Identify spending patterns. For understanding where money goes (not budgeting app - observation tool).

### 43. Tool Inventory
List tools you own. Note when borrowed/lent. Track maintenance needs. Suggest tools to acquire or get rid of. For tool stewardship.

### 44. Buy/Don't Buy Decision Logger
Considering purchase? Log the want. Agent asks questions (need vs. want, can borrow, will use, can afford). Revisit in a week. For thoughtful consumption.

### 45. Skill Value Calculator
Track hours learning new skills. Estimate value of work you can now do yourself vs. hiring. For understanding return on learning.

### 46. Subscription Auditor
Track all subscriptions. Calculate annual cost. Agent flags unused ones. For conscious spending.

## Sustainable Living & Craft (8 projects)

### 47. Repair Log
Track items repaired vs. replaced. Note difficulty, cost, time. Build repair knowledge base. For right-to-repair practice.

### 48. Local Food Sourcer
Track farmers markets, CSAs, local producers. Seasonal availability. Agent suggests weekly meal plans from local sources. For eating locally.

### 49. Clothing Tracker
Photo every clothing item. Track wear frequency. Note repairs needed. Suggests outfits. For conscious wardrobes and fewer purchases.

### 50. Craft Project Tracker
Track knitting, woodworking, pottery projects. Materials used. Techniques learned. Time invested. Photo progress. For makers.

### 51. Water/Energy Usage Monitor
Log utility bills. Track usage patterns. Identify anomalies. Set reduction goals. For resource awareness.

### 52. Zero Waste Tracker
Track trash/recycling/compost volume. Note sources of waste. Agent suggests reductions. For environmental mindfulness.

### 53. Seed Library
Track seeds saved, viability, planting notes. Share with local gardeners. Agent suggests planting schedule. For seed savers.

### 54. Preservation Logger
Track canning, fermenting, preserving. Recipe notes. Storage life. Success rates. For food preservation.

## Meta-Tools (Tools for Building Tools) (6 projects)

### 55. Project Template Generator
You've built 20 personal tools. Agent analyzes common patterns. Generates templates for new tools. For efficient building.

### 56. Tool Maintenance Dashboard
All your personal tools in one place. Check when last updated. Note what needs fixing. Schedule maintenance. For sustainable tool ecosystems.

### 57. Agent Prompt Library
Collect effective prompts for different tasks. Tag by use case. Share with others. Fork and improve. For prompt craft.

### 58. Building Pattern Documenter
Document how you build. Your classes, your methodology. Export as guide. For teaching others your process.

### 59. Tool Usage Analytics
Which personal tools do you actually use? How often? What's unused? Agent suggests what to deprecate or combine. For honest assessment.

### 60. Portfolio Generator
Showcase tools you've built. Document process. Export to website. For sharing your work.

## Selection Criteria

These projects share characteristics:

✅ **Include:**
- Serve real personal needs
- Could extend to small groups (family, friends)
- Integrate with physical world
- Support learning, craft, sustainability
- Enable sharing and teaching
- Build over time with real use

❌ **Exclude:**
- Purely digital optimization
- Scaling to thousands of users
- Monetization platforms
- Social media clones
- Gamification/streaks
- CEO porn (managing virtual teams)

## Project vs. Practice

**Practice (Weeks 1-13):**
- Completable in a week
- Learn one concept
- One tool
- Hypothetical OK (learning)

**Project (Weeks 14+):**
- Multi-week
- Integrate concepts
- Real use required
- Must serve actual need

Examples:
- **Practice:** Build a link catcher (Week 5)
- **Project:** Build personal research system integrating links, notes, and synthesis (Week 18)
